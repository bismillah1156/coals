About Us
Our coal contains a calorific value of approximately above 4,500 cal/gm. Also, supply a quality product for the market. Our company is privately organised, have the capacity to produce 2000+ tons of pure local coal per day based on 100+ employees and was established in 2013. Our vision is to be a recognized and qualified coal mining company focused on delivering on our commitments for excellence and valuing and developing our country and employees.

Also improve our society life style by create a large job opportunity for them. Also we oriented contribution for our country development as much as possible and change the country lack of aquality coal material.


Our Singularity

  1. Zero Harm - Zero harm to our employees, the environment and communities is our goal.
  2. Dignity and Respect – Equal treatment and opportunity for all employees with transparent processes founded on dignity and respect and encouraging a sense of ownership.
  3. Sustainability – Sustainable growth supported by a safe, profitable and socially responsible business while developing long-term resources.
  4. Collaboration – Productive and ethical working relationships, transparent and responsive dialogue with surrounding communities and public agencies for the benefit of all stakeholders.
  5. Excellence – A commitment to focus on a culture of the best business practices.
